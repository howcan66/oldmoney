SECTION 6: TOOLTIP ANIMATION & TIMING
================================================================================

TIMING GUIDELINES:

DELAY (Before appearance):
  Desktop: 0-500ms delay (prevents flashing)
  Tablet: 300-500ms (press-and-hold alternative)
  Mobile: No delay (immediate on tap)

FADE-IN DURATION:
  Desktop: 200-300ms (smooth appearance)
  Tablet: 200ms (responsive feel)
  Mobile: Instant or 100ms (fast feedback)

PERSISTENCE:
  Desktop: Hide on mouseout
  Tablet: Persist until next interaction (tap elsewhere)
  Mobile: Persist until second tap or outside tap

EXAMPLE CSS ANIMATION:

.tooltip {
  opacity: 0;
  transition: opacity 0.2s ease-in-out;
  pointer-events: none;
}

.tooltip.visible {
  opacity: 1;
  pointer-events: auto;
}
