CSV + Email Workflow (Interest Calculator)

1) Open the calculator:
   - interestoninterest.html

2) Enter values:
   - Saving Start Amount
   - Yearly Average Interest
   - Monthly Savings
   - Yearly Deposit

3) Click Calculate.
   - Results tables and summary appear.

CSV Export:
4) Click "Export to CSV" to download a CSV file.
   - The file includes all 40 years.

Email Results:
5) Enter recipient email in the Email Results field.
6) Click "Send Email".
   - Requires serverless email function configured.

Serverless Email Setup (Netlify):
7) Set environment variables:
   - SENDGRID_API_KEY
   - SENDGRID_FROM (verified sender)
8) Run locally with Netlify CLI:
   netlify dev
9) The endpoint used is:
   /.netlify/functions/send-email

Notes:
- Email sends summary + CSV content in plain text.
- If not configured, "Send Email" will show an error.
